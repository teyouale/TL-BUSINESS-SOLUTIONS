About us

TL Business Solutions(TLBS)   has a unique value added services which encourage clients to be comfortable enough to share their business ambitions. We simply listen for the core goals of the clients needs and work with them. We help them structure the goals and break them down into practical tasks on a daily basis. The biggest fears for our clients are: not knowing where to start from, who is the right expert to hire and trust, can the consultant really help deliver on my (the clients) goals, how can they get financed and will they be able to payback on time or even question if their finances are enough to start their business. So many questions, we as TLBS work on answering by providing well tested and proven result based solutions. We don’t reject the say “ Rome was not built in a day”. but compliment it by our believe that achieving your daily day’s tasks brings you closer to you dream business and tomorrow becomes a clearer picture of what you dream was and has become. “Take steps not leaps” is our core principle to achieve your business goals.
